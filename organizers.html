Brief CVs of the organizers

Shan Huang recevied the M.S. degree in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2015. She is currently a researcher at Tencent. Hercurrentresearchinterestsincludeoptical character recognition and multimodal machine learning.
Shen Huang received the Ph.D. degree in the Institution of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2014. He is currently a researcher at Tencent. His current research interests include speech recognition and optical character recognition.
Pengfei Hu received the B.S. degree in Electronic commerce from Beijing Information Science and Technology University, Beijing, China, in 2008 and the Ph.D. degree in the Institution of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2014. He is currently a researcher at Tencent. His current research interests include speech recognition and audio classfication.
Xiang Wang received the B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electronic engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2014 and 2019, respectively. He is currently a researcher at Tencent. His research interests are computer vision and machine learning, with particular interests in image restoration, semantic segmentation and weakly- supervised learning. He serves as a reviewer for TIP, CVPR, ICCV, ICLR and AAAI.
Jian Kang received the B.S. degree in electronic engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2013 and the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2018. He is currently a researcher at Tencent. His current research interests include speech recognition and optical character recognition.
Lianwen Jin received the B.S. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China, Anhui, China, and the Ph.D. degree from the South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 1991 and 1996, respectively. He is a professor at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering at the South China University of Technology. His research interests include Optical Character Recognition, Document Analysis and Recognition, Scene Text Detection and Recognition, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. He has authored over 200 scientific papers. He received the New Century Excellent Talent Program of MOE Award and the Guangdong Pearl River Distinguished Professor Award in 2006 and 2011, respectively.
YuLiang Liu received the B.S. degree in electronic and information engineering from the South China University of Technology, China, in 2016, where he received the Ph.D. degree with Deep Learning and Vision Computing Lab (DLVC-Lab), under the supervision of Prof. Lianwen Jin. He is now a postdoc in University of Adelaide, under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen. He is working on scene text understanding, handwritten character recognition, document analysis, and deep learning-based text detection and recognition.
